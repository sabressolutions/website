About SABRES SOLUTIONS

Founded by Dr. Sabrina Restrepo, a senior biopharmaceutical executive with more than 15 years of leadership experience in global manufacturing, quality systems, and operational transformation, SABRES SOLUTIONS partners with organizations to strengthen compliance, optimize operations, and accelerate the delivery of life-saving therapies.

Dr. Restrepo has led large global organizations and complex technical initiatives across multiple manufacturing sites, supporting vaccines, biologics, and advanced therapies. Her experience spans the full product lifecycle—from development and technology transfer to commercialization and supply continuity—while ensuring alignment with regulatory expectations and enterprise strategy.

Throughout her career, she has successfully delivered large-scale initiatives that improved manufacturing performance, protected critical supply chains, and strengthened global quality systems. Her leadership has enabled the delivery of millions of vaccine doses worldwide, safeguarded hundreds of millions of dollars in product supply, and driven operational transformations across global organizations.
